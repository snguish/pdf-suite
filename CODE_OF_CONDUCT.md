# Code of conduct

## Our standard

PDF Suite contributors and maintainers are expected to create a welcoming, professional environment. Be respectful, assume good intent, accept constructive feedback, and focus discussion on improving the project.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, publication of private information, and other abusive conduct are not acceptable.

## Scope

This standard applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project spaces, as well as when representing the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately where appropriate, and respectful of the safety and privacy of everyone involved.
